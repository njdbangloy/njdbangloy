About Us

Meskie's Ethiopian Restaurant, nestled in the heart of Berkeley, is a culinary haven that brings the rich and diverse flavors of Ethiopia to the Bay Area. With a commitment to authenticity and a passion for sharing the cultural heritage of Ethiopia, we offer a unique dining experience. Our menu features a delightful array of traditional Ethiopian dishes, carefully prepared with a blend of aromatic spices and locally-sourced ingredients. Whether you're a seasoned Ethiopian food enthusiast or new to this culinary journey, Meskie's invites you to savor the vibrant tastes and warm hospitality that define our restaurant. Join us to explore the tastes, textures, and traditions of Ethiopia in the welcoming ambiance of our beloved Berkeley establishment.
